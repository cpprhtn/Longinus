# Who it's for & what you get

↑ back to [README](../README.md) · doc map: [why](why.md) · [ethics](ethics.md) · [usage](usage.md)

## Who it's for

Anyone, regardless of security background:

- **Solo devs & founders** auditing their own (often AI-generated) app before launch.
- **Bug-bounty hunters & pentesters** running a repeatable methodology against authorized scope.
- **AI/LLM app builders** red-teaming prompt injection, agent over-permissioning, RAG leakage.
- **CTF players & learners** drilling a specific category.

## What you get out of it

A report, not a scanner dump:

- An **executive summary** + a **prioritized fix list**.
- Per finding: **severity (CVSS 4.0)**, **exact location** (`file:line` or endpoint),
  **reproducible PoC**, **impact**, and a **code-level remediation**.
- Low false positives by design: *prove it or park it.*
